Welcome to Cheesecake!

We are a family-owned business that has been serving delicious cheesecake for over 50 years. Our cheesecake is made with the finest ingredients and baked fresh daily.

About Us :

Our story began in 1969 when our founder, John Smith, started baking cheesecake in his home kitchen. He quickly gained a reputation for making the best cheesecake in town, and soon opened his first Cheesecake shop.
